Free Dog

  • High Concept: You are a free roaming dog who just wants to pee on fire hydrants; be careful to avoid dog catchers.
  • Player Experience Goals: Laugh at the ridiculousness of the game concept.
  • Genre: Basic platforming/collecting
  • Game play: As a dog, the left and right arrow keys control running left and right. The Spacebar is used to jump. The up key is used to unleash a golden fury upon the fire hydrants of the world. Ideally, the game would have had more challenge with additional spawning dog-catchers.
  • Prototype goal: Gain some familiarity with javascript, phaser, and animation.
  • Features: Color-changing fire hydrants
  • Setting/Premise: You are a dog. Dogs pee on things, especially fire hydrants.
  • Target Audience: People with nothing better to do with their time.
  • Play time: Maybe a minute
  • Strategies: Jump over the dog catcher when he comes.
